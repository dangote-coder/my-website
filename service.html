SERVICES OFFERED

Event Ushers: Professional ushers are assigned to greet guests, provide directions, assist with seating arrangement, and ensure a seamless flow of the event.

Event Planning & Coordination: The agency often works in close coordination with event planners, offering logistical support such as crowd control, managing queues, and providing assistance to VIP guests.

Hospitality Support: Ushers are trained to handle customer service tasks, ensuring that attenders are well-informed and comfortable throughout the event.

VIP and High-Profile Event Management: Specializing in events requiring a more personalized level of attention, the Agency offers dedicated staff for VIP guests and high-profile events.

Other Services Include;

  • Brand Activation/Promotional Ushering/Award Bearers
  • Waiting services/Party Supporters/In-House Emceeing
  • Bridesmaids Support/Dowry Wrapping & Carrying/Traditional Dance
